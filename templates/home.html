大家好! Welcome, everyone to my final project for CS50. 💻

"Zhournalism" (a portmanteau of "zhongwen" (中文; Mandarin for "Chinese") and "journalism") is a novel study tool designed to make studying the latest news headlines engaging and easy. The tool scrapes the latest headlines, identifies any HSK* vocabulary therein, and allows the user to interact with the text: 🐭 mousing over the word will display the Pinyin, and a 🎯 click reveals the English translation(s).

*HSK (Hanyu Shuiping Kaoshi; 汉语水平考试), literally "Chinese Level Exam", is a standardized set of 5000 Chinese characters, and is often used as a measure of a student's Mandarin level. There are currently 6 levels. This is slated to expand to 9 levels in 2022.
